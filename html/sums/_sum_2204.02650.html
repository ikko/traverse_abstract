Spatio-Temporal Dynamic Graph Relation Learning for Urban Metro Flow Prediction

Researchers develop a spatio-temporal dynamic graph relational learning model (STDGRL) to predict urban metro station ﬂow. Extensive experiments have been conducted based on metro data in Beijing, Shanghai, Chongqing and Hangzhou. The research is published in the Journal of LATEX CLASS FILES, VOL. 14, NO. 8, August 14, 2015. The authors propose a graph relationship learning module to learn dynamic spatial relationships between metro stations without a graph adjacency matrix. They also provide a transformer-based long-term relationship prediction module.
